CCSCNE

I am a board member and an active participant in the Consortium for Computing Sciences in Colleges, Northeastern Region (CCSCNE). I was co-organizer of the panels, workshops, and tutorials for the 2004 CCSCNE conference held at Union College, co-chair for for undergraduate posters for the 2005 CCSCNE conference, held at Providence College, and co-chair for papers for the 2006 CCSCNE conference held at Holy Cross. I was the co-chair for papers once again for the 2007 CCSCNE conference held at R.I.T., and served in the same role for the 2008 CCSCNE conference held at Wagner College. I was a co-chair for panels, tutorials, and workshops for the 2009 CCSCNE conference held at SUNY Plattsburgh. I was a co-chair for papers for the 2012 CCSCNE conference held at Quinnipiac University, for the 2013 CCSCNE conference held at Siena College.

ADAPT '03

I was a co-organizer for ADAPT '03, a conference held at RPI in October, 2003, in honor of my Ph.D. advisor, Joe Flaherty, and I was co-editor of the proceedings volume, which appeared as a special issue of Applied Numerical Mathematics in February 2005.
